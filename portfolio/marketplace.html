    CTL Marketplace Project Overview

    The CTL Marketplace provides a selection of evidence-based, multi-tiered mathematics and literacy products designed for students in grades K-8. Registered customers benefit from streamlined purchasing via user accounts. Administrative users possess the capability to manage orders, reconcile credit card transactions, and generate comprehensive sales reports.

    CTL's former sales process, which relied on a complex PDF form and a disconnected university credit card payment website, proved inadequate for handling increased sales volume. This resulted in a poor customer experience and left administrators without the necessary reporting and reconciliation capabilities for credit card transactions. The solution involved implementing an e-commerce Drupal site featuring a custom payment gateway, customer accounts, and administrative reporting tools.

    This strategic platform upgrade positions the CTL Marketplace for long-term growth and enhanced user experience. Through a robust e-commerce Drupal platform, educators and administrators now have access to streamlined purchasing, secure payment processing, and insightful sales analytics.

    Key Deliverables

    UI Design

    Following industry best practices for e-commerce design, the UI development prioritized an intuitive and accessible user experience. This resulted in the creation of a streamlined checkout process, detailed product and item pages, and clear, automated email receipts.

    Web Development

    The UI designs were implemented as a fully functional Drupal Commerce web application, encompassing customer accounts, administrative tools, and detailed product pages. A custom module integrated a payment gateway, connecting the marketplace to the university's credit card API. Rigorous testing validated functionality and performance across diverse devices and browsers, including dedicated accessibility testing for WCAG compliance. Ongoing support and maintenance ensured post-launch stability and user satisfaction.

    Content Coordination

    Through active collaboration with stakeholders, product content and media were systematically collected and organized using a collaborative project management tool. Consistent data capture was achieved through the development of standardized templates, and product copy was refined through multiple rounds of peer review.
